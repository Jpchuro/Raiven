ABOUT US

"TVL ICT" stands for "Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Information and Communications Technology." This is a strand under the TVL track in senior high school in the Philippines, focused on providing students with technical skills related to the field of ICT. The TVL ICT strand equips students with knowledge and competencies in areas such as:
•Computer programming
•Web development
•Animation
•Computer hardware servicing •Networking

This strand prepares students for jobs in the tech industry or further studies in ICT-related fields. It is ideal for students who are interested in careers in technology, software development, or IT support.
